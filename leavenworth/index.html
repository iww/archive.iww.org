General Defense Committee of the IWW Communique, undated, probably circa 1920. GIF images totalling about 112Kb. Includes header and text, each maximized for quality and minimized for bandwidth. You'll recognize some of the names from the gallery below, and be utterly appalled at what the defense was reduced to for means of communication... Posted 7/19/1999.

Sentences Bourne by IWW Prisoners at Leavenworth. Suspected by web admin to be a partial list, from a pamphlet called "The I.W.W. Prisoners," bearing the date 1922 and in support of the prisoners' campaign for release, but not bearing the markings of IWW lit (seal or preamble). But then, there may be some missing pages. Posted 7/99.
