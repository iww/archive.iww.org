About the IWW

Official IWW Literature and Documents

Official IWW literature is any literature, written by or about the IWW, that has been approved by the IWW's General Executive Board (who are elected to one-year terms by a democratic vote of the dues paying membership), or by a direct vote of the IWW's general membership. The following documents are official literature, but is not an exhaustive set.
