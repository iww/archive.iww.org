Labor Day 2002

Despite searing heat, over two dozen people came out to march with the Los Angeles Wobblies in the annual Labor Day parade in Wilmington, CA. The march of over 5,000 local union folks and their families was spirited in the face of many union disputes brewing.



Some of our signs read: "Solidaridad Obrera - IWW", "End Wage Slavery", "An Injury to One is an Injury to All", "Fire Your Boss". The other unions liked our "Repeal Taft-Hartley" sign the most, as the Taft-Hartley Act is threatening to bust many unions in the LA area.
