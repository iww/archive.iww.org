Submissions to Anarcho-Syndicalist Review:

The editorial collective of ASR welcomes submissions--including articles, artwork, graphics, photographs, letters to the editor, and critical reviews--from our readers, as long as they are relevant to the mission of this publication.

If you wish to submit written material for publication, you may:

  • Email them to (please include the text in the body of the message, and also attach a copy of the file if possible, to preserve any special formatting).

  • Mail us your submission(s) as file(s) on 3.5" floppy or compact disk.

  • Mail us typewritten hardcopy of your submission(s).

Please do not send handwritten submissions. We cannot return any submissions you send us, so please make a copy of each submission for youself.

You may also submit graphic material including pictures by any of the methods listed above, but please send camera ready material. Web based graphics are acceptable for this website, but often they are not suitable for publication in hard copy because the resolution is too low.

Editorial Policy:

We reserve the right to edit submissions for clarity, brevity, style, and grammer as we see fit. We may also amend your submissions as accuracy warrants. We do not guarantee publication of any submission.

If you wish to send us a submission, but do not wish your materials to be published, please write "Not for Publication" on your hard copy or disks, or indicate that what you send is not intended for publication in the body of your email message.
