Help Us Distribute Anarcho-Syndicalist Review

ASR is available to bookstores and distributors, or to individuals and groups for tabling.

Bookstores can obtain ASR through our distributor (Ubiquity), or we will furnish copies directly on a fully returnable basis (minimum order 3 copies). If you prefer to work with a distributor that does not currently carry ASR, we would be glad to work with you to persuade them to add ASR to their list.

We encourage readers to approach their favorite local bookstore or newsstand and ask them to carry ASR.
