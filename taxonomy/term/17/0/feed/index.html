For an overview of the IU 330's history and contact information, please visit our homepage. en Statement by C.W.O.C in Solidarity with Jimmy Johns Workers https://www.iww.org/node/5191 <p><img src="/graphics/IU330/IU330Logo2color.jpg" align="right" />The Construction Workers Organizing Committee of the Industrial Workers of the World wishes to express our deepest solidarity with the Jimmy Johns Workers union. As union construction workers, we understand that the only way to maintain and raise both our wages and working conditions is to show solidarity with not only workers of the various trades within construction; but also with workers of other industries. Solidarity means having a true understanding of the old saying, <b>An injury to one is an injury to all</b>.</p> <p>Solidarity is also the action that springs from this understanding. The greatest action of solidarity that one can follow is the exercising of the <b>right to not cross any picket lines</b>.</p> <p>Everyone knows that times are hard. The construction industry has been hit especially hard, and many brothers and sisters in the trades are not working. This means that what little work we get, we desperately need. However, as construction workers; we also understand that <b>in these hard times, we must act and be stronger than ever before!</b> For hundreds of years, construction worker have fought long and hard battles, through many recessions, to reach the level of pay and the amount of power we have on the job. We certainly should not let this recession destroy the progress we have made.</p> <p>The Construction Workers Organizing Committee and the construction workers of the IWW promise to continue to not cross picket lines! No matter what union or what industry, we will continue to act out solidarity and exercise our rights under <u>section 7 of the NLRA</u>: to act in concert with fellow workers to uphold the best interest of the working class. We urge all construction workers to do the same!</p> <p><b>Who we are:</b> The Construction Workers Organizing Committee or CWOC is a subordinate body of the Industrial Workers of the World. Our aim is to organize ALL construction workers into ONE democratic fighting union.
