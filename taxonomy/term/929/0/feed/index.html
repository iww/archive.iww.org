Industrial Workers of the World - Kentucky IWW https://www.iww.org/taxonomy/term/929/0 Kentucky IWW en KY IWW: Now Accepting Dues On-line https://www.iww.org/content/ky-iww-now-accepting-dues-line <p><strong>FW Patrick, Press Secretary - <a href="http://kyiww.org/2015/08/04/ky-iww-now-accepting-dues-on-line/" target="_blank">Kentucky IWW</a>, August 4, 2015</strong></p> <p>After much tinkering, the KY IWW GMB has developed a way to collect dues on-line. We feel this is an easy way for those members who are from far-off, or simply unable to attend certain meeting days, to remain part of the One Big Union.</p> <p>You can follow the link through our &ldquo;Dues and Membership&rdquo; page to pay dues now or in the future. The link there will direct you to an external page where you can select your dues range and remain a member in good standing.</p> <p>We&rsquo;re also hopeful that we&rsquo;ll have more options through the page in the future, including ways to join the KY IWW on-line, donate to the branch, purchase branch stamps, and so on.</p> <p>One (or two) final thing(s): notes from the July meeting&ndash;including a long think piece on movement music&ndash;are forthcoming. Stayed tuned for that. And finally, be sure to join us for the KY IWW GMB open house THIS SATURDAY,&nbsp;8:00 p.m., at our space in The Mammoth.
